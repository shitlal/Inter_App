CGTSI invites Proposals / Requests for Workshops / Seminars

With a view to creating awareness and familiarizing users / beneficiaries about the Credit Guarantee Scheme, CGTSI invites proposals for conducting or for participation in workshops / seminars at different locations in the country.  CGTSI would be happy to support and participate in all such events.  The important considerations / requirements to be kept in view by the organizers or those inviting CGTSI are :

 

1.                 The participants should be mainly from banks, SSI / tiny units, industry associations, consultants, etc. and of reasonable number (at least 50)

2.                 Banks can exclusively organize workshops for their officers in their training centres / colleges or include sessions on CGTSI scheme in their ongoing programmes.

3.                 Facilities for power-point presentation and projection may preferably be arranged.  Where such facilities cannot be provided the overhead projector (OHP) may be arranged.

4.                 Duration of CGTSI session can be from about 30 minutes to two hours depending upon the need and target group.

5.                 Locations known for clusters of small scale units may be preferred.

6.                 Involvement of a local small industry association would make the effort more meaningful.

7.                   Workshops / Seminars organised by industry associations for SSI / tiny units can be considered for limited financial support on the basis of merits of the proposal.

8.                   A notice of ten (10) days may be given to CGTSI to enable it to prepare itself and suitably depute the officials.

The lending institution desirous to register with CGTSI may submit an undertaking on a stamp paper of appropriate value to the registered office of the Trust.

The following documents may be furnished in addition to the undertaking for registration purpose:

a) Annual reports for the preceding 3 years.

b) List of offices along with their addresses designated to operate the Credit Guarantee scheme with CGTSI.

c) Name and designation of the designated officers to operate the scheme, together with their signatures.

Note: -

Items b) and c) above may be provided subsequent to the registration of the lending institution, if so desired by the lending institution.

Who can register:-

Only scheduled commercial banks are presently eligible to register with CGTSI for operating the credit guarantee scheme.

Regional Rural Banks desirous to register with CGTSI may send in complete details about their performance including:

·  Annual Reports for the last 3 years

·  Details of geographical areas of operation

·  Average loan size

·  Recovery performance, % of standard assets to total assets, Capital adequacy ratio.

Eligible Borrowers

New as well as existing manufacturing SSI units including IT and software industry, to which credit facility up to Rs.10 lakh (term loan as well as working capital) has been provided by eligible institutions without any collateral including third party guarantee. The borrower should preferably avail of credit facility from single eligible institution. However, where the credit facility is obtained from more than one institution, the total liability of borrower shall not exceed Rs.10 lakh.

Annual Service fee        

An annual service charge of one per cent on the outstanding credit facilities of the borrowal accounts covered under the Scheme as on March 31 of each year, shall be paid by the lender on or before 30th April of every year. For example, annual service on the outstanding credit facilities as on March 31, 2001, shall be payable by April 30, 2001.

      The lender may pass on the incidence of guarantee fee and annual service fee to the borrower. Depending upon balance outstanding and in the normal course, on a five year credit facility, on an annualised basis, the guarantee fee and service charge payable would be about 1% per cent per annum.

The Trust reserves the right to revise the guarantee fee   annual service fee from time to time.

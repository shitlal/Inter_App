RECRUITMENT

Consultants- Finance, Marketing (Ref: CGTMSE)

To meet the growing volume in business, CGTMSE is looking for candidates in the age group of 25-30 years with suitable qualification viz., CA / MBA (Finance & Marketing) / CFA and post graduate in Economics / Commerce having 3-4 years post qualification experience preferably in insurance / financial sector. All candidates should have working knowledge of IT systems and possess good communication skills. The operations of CGTMSE is web-based and work involves managing large volume of transactions, liaisoning with banks and member lending institutions / Government Departments, treasury management, conducting training programmes / workshops, evaluating risks, managing claims payments, press & public relations, inspection, data analysis & research etc. (6 posts on contractual basis at Mumbai)

All the Associate Institutions/subsidiaries of SIDBI offer a competitive and attractive remuneration package. Remuneration would be comparable to industry norms and would not be a constraint for the right candidate. The above jobs entail basic computer proficiency and familiarity with MS Office. The number of vacancies indicated above can vary based on actual requirement. Applications should be made quoting the reference number in the subject line of the mail/ covering letter accompanied by two recent photographs, CV and name of two references.

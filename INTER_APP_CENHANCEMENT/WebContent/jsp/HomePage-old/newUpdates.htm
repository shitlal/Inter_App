Zero Risk weights and Provisioning for guaranteed portion of the loan

In terms of the RBI Circular dated June 07, 2001, SSI advances guaranteed by CGTSI will attract zero risk weight for the guaranteed portion and that in case the advances covered by CGTSI become non-performing, no provision needs to be made for the guaranteed portion.

Guarantee fee :

A one time guarantee fee of 2.5 per cent of the credit sanctioned without collateral security shall be paid upfront to the Trust by the Member Lending Institution (MLI) for seeking guarantee cover.  The guarantee fee is to be paid within 30 days from the date of first disbursement of credit extended by the MLI to a borrower.

Annual Service fee:

Guarantee cover extended by CGTSI in respect of any specific borrower shall be valid provided the MLI concerned pays an annual service fee of one per cent, on the outstanding credit of the borrowal account, as on March 31 of every year. Such annual service fee is to be paid by the MLI on or before 31st May of that year. For example, annual service on the outstanding credit facilities as on March 31, 2003, shall be payable by May 31, 2003.

The Trust reserves the right to revise the guarantee fee / annual service fee from time to time.

Cost to the borrower:

The credit guarantee scheme leaves it to the discretion of the MLIs to decide about passing on the incidence of guarantee fee and annual service fee to the borrower or alternatively they may decide to bear it themselves.  In the normal course, on a credit facility of 5 years of tenure, the guarantee fee and annual service fee payable would work out to less than 1.5% on an annualised basis.

Interest Rate payable by Borrower :

The MLI shall follow the guidelines issued by RBI. However, interest rate shall not exceed 3 per cent over and above the Prime Lending Rate of the MLI, excluding the annual service fee.

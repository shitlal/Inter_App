Announcement

 

Interactive Workshop for Bankers on
Credit Guarantee Scheme (CGS) of CGTSI
The feedback received through our interaction with various stakeholders and through other channels indicates lack of awareness about CGS in general, as the main reason for low off-take. Another reason brought to the notice of CGTSI is inadequate familiarity of officials of member-banks with the modalities of CGS. With a view to addressing these major constraints and to facilitate familiarization with procedures, requirements, etc. under CGS, CGTSI is organizing a half-day workshop on September 30, 2003 (Tuesday) at New Delhi exclusively for officials of Banks.

 

Venue:          

Amaltash

 

 

India Habitat Centre (IHC)

1000 hrs. to 1400 hrs

 

 

 

The focus of the workshop will be on highlighting the advantages of CGS, procedural modalities including online lodgement of application, claim settlement, etc. It is intended to utilize the opportunity to allay any concern regarding the scheme. CGTSI would be happy to enlist new lending institutions (scheduled commercial banks, select RRBs and other lending institutions approved by Govt. of India) as its MLIs. CMD, SIDBI who is also the Chairman of CGTSI, Development Commissioner (SSI) & Additional Secretary to Govt. of India, who is also the Vice-Chairman of CGTSI will briefly address the participants. The workshop will be followed by lunch. There is no participation fee.

 
Banks are requested make use of the opportunity and depute senior functionaries engaged in handling the Priority Sector / Credit Department or Advances to SSI units. In addition to senior officials nomination of more participants preferably those operating at the branch / field level is welcome. Please feel free to contact CGTSI in this regard. The name, designation and other particulars of the official(s) nominated to participate in the workshop may please be conveyed to CGTSI in the enclosed format latest by September 25, 2003

Modifications in the Credit Guarantee Scheme

 

 

 In terms of Circular No. 36 / 2007-2008 dated July 02, 2007, the coverage of the scheme has been extended to all new and existing Micro and Small Enterprises ( both in the manufacturing sector as well as in the service sector).  In this connection, in respect of  Micro and Small Enterprises (in the  service sector), you are advised to adhere to the RBI’s guidelines on ‘ Lending to Priority Sector ‘ ie.  include small road and water transport operatiors, small business, professional and self employed persons and  all other service enterprises under the ambit of Micro and Small Enterprises but exclude retail trade. You are requested to advise your operating offices (Zonal / Branch Offices) accordingly in this regard.

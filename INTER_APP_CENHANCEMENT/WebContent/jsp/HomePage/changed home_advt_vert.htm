Important Instructions for MLIs regarding ASF for FY 2008

 

 

1.                              Demand Draft (DD) for ASF should be routed only through the Coordinating Office.  NO direct remittance will be accepted from Branch Office / assisted enterprise.

 

2.                              Late receipt of DDs (i.e. after May 31, 2008) will NOT be accepted under any circumstances.

 

3.                              DD amount should exactly match with the amount indicated in the SF DAN(s).  Any discrepancy in amount / unit should be sent with a reconciliation sheet and payment by separate DD.

 

4.                              Allocation of ASF should be made against individual units in Receipts & Payment module. [Maximum 50 allocations in each RP as system will not accept more than 50 SFDANs in each RP]

 

5.                              DD for SFDAN should not be clubbed with payment of Guarantee Fee.  Separate DDs may be sent for the same.

 

 

N.B.:    (i)         ASF for Closed and Expired accounts have  not  been  generated.   ASF  

Demand Advice is generated only for live cases as on March 31, 2008.

 

(ii)        Generation / Raising of ASF (ASF 20008) advices would not entitle any right to Member Lending Institution for lodgement of claims towards guarantee cover, that may be lodged in future, in respect of their assisted units [case(s)] for which payment of ASF for any of the previous years was not paid / not paid in time.
